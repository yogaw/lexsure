Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lexsure.

What's "Liability Check"?

It's our first tool that lets you upload legal documents and instantly understand what liabilities you face—and what rights you can claim.

Are these results legally binding?

No. Think of them as trusted guidance. Verified results by our legal partners carry more weight and clarity but still don't replace courtroom litigation.

How does legal credit work?

Legal credits are tokens you can use to request expert verification, faster turnarounds, or access premium features.

How secure is my data?

We use legal-grade encryption and strict access controls. Your documents are stored securely and never shared with third parties without your explicit permission.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your purchased credits will remain valid until used or until their expiration date.
